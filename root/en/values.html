What's Best in the World

Our core values guide everything we do

Climate Awareness

We use only sustainable, natural materials and eco-friendly dyes. Our production process minimizes waste and energy use.

Safe Childhood for All

We donate 5% of profits to organizations supporting children's rights worldwide.

Anti-Racism & Anti-Sexism

We actively work against all forms of discrimination in our business practices and community engagement.

LGBTQIA+ Support

Our pride collection supports LGBTQIA+ organizations, and we offer custom shawls in all pride colors.

Fair Wages

When we collaborate with other artisans, we ensure they receive fair compensation for their work.

Community

We host free workshops for marginalized communities to learn weaving skills.

Special Collections

Our values-inspired shawls let you wear your heart on your sleeve (or around your shoulders!)
