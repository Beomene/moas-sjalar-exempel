Welcome to Fylgjia!

Every carrier shawl from me has been handmade here in my weaving studio in Gothenburg. When I became a mother to Liv and Eva, I discovered how amazing carrier shawls are. If you'd like to try out what it feels like to use carrier shawls, you can reach out to me. If you're three or more people who want to book a workshop, we can meet at your place. Children are of course welcome too - they're the ones who are meant to be carried!

Reach out with any questions and I'll reply soon.
