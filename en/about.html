About Moa's Shawls

My journey with weaving began when I was expecting my first child. I wanted to create something beautiful and functional that would help me carry my baby close to my heart.

Each shawl is handwoven in my Gothenburg studio using natural, sustainable materials. I carefully select every thread to ensure both beauty and durability.

The patterns I create are inspired by Scandinavian nature and the changing seasons. Many of my designs also incorporate symbols of the values I hold dear - equality, diversity, and environmental consciousness.

When you purchase a shawl from me, you're not just getting a baby carrier - you're getting a piece of wearable art that's been created with love and intention.
